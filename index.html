Research Overview & Technology Transfer

Founded a tech company after undergraduate studies, later served as research assistant and pursued M.S. degree. Main research focuses on intelligent NDT using deep learning for ultrasonic, MFL, and visual inspection. Successfully developed intelligent NDT robots for weld seam tracking, defect recognition, and wall-climbing, with several patents transferred to enterprises. Currently principal investigator in multiple provincial/key projects and promote intelligent upgrading of special equipment inspection.
